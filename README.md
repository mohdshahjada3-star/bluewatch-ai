## 🎯 Selected SDG — SDG 14: Life Below Water

Marine pollution is one of the most serious 
environmental challenges of our time. 
Oceans cover 71% of our planet but are being 
destroyed by plastic waste, oil spills, sewage, 
and industrial pollutants every single day.

---

## ❌ The Problem

- 8 million tons of plastic enters oceans every year
- Oil spills destroy marine ecosystems instantly
- Sewage discharge kills aquatic life
- Authorities are SLOW to respond because 
  pollution is detected too late
- No easy way for common people to report 
  pollution incidents
- Coastal communities and fishermen suffer 
  economic losses

**Delayed detection = wider damage = more deaths**

---

## ✅ Our Solution — BlueWatch AI

BlueWatch AI allows ANYONE to report marine 
pollution instantly using just a photo.

The AI analyzes the image, identifies the 
pollution type, generates a risk score, and 
immediately alerts authorities — all in seconds.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 AI Pollution Detection
  Upload any water pollution image and Claude AI 
  instantly identifies the pollution type and risk

- 🗺️ Interactive Pollution Map
  See all pollution hotspots across India on a 
  real-time heat map

- 📊 Risk Score Analysis
  Every report gets a score from 0-100
  Low / Medium / High risk classification

- 🚨 Automated Alert System
  High risk pollution triggers immediate alerts 
  to NGOs and government authorities

- 👮 Authority Dashboard
  Authorities can review, verify and update 
  cleanup status of every report

- 📍 Auto Geolocation
  Automatically captures location of pollution

- 🔐 User Authentication
  Secure login system with role-based access
